## What You're About To Do

Learn how to work with other people on code. This is the real superpower — collaboration is how everything from apps to games to websites gets built.

## How Teams Work on GitHub

### Fork = Your Own Copy
See a project you like? Fork it.

1. Go to any public repo (try [this one](https://github.com/skills/introduction-to-github))
2. Click **Fork** in the top right
3. Now you have your own copy to mess with

![Fork button](assets/screenshots/06-fork-button.png)

You can change anything in your fork without affecting the original.

### Pull Request = "Hey Check This Out"
Made something cool in your fork? Send it back.

1. Make your changes in your fork
2. Click **Contribute** → **Open pull request**
3. Describe what you did
4. Submit it

The original author reviews your work and can merge it in. That's how open source works — millions of people building together.

### Star = Show Love ⭐
See a repo you think is cool? Star it. It's like a bookmark + a shoutout. The creator sees it.
